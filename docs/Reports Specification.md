# Reports Specification

## Daily, Monthly, and Annual Reports

---

## 1. Purpose of Reports

This document defines all **operational, administrative, and statistical reports** required by the Transport Unit Management System. Reports support daily decision-making, performance monitoring, compliance, and long-term planning.

Reports are categorized by **time period**:

* Daily Reports (operational control)
* Monthly Reports (management oversight)
* Annual Reports (strategic planning)

---

# 2. Daily Reports

Daily reports focus on **real-time operations, attendance accuracy, and transport efficiency**.

---

## 2.1 Daily Attendance Summary Report

**Purpose:**
Provide a high-level view of daily attendance performance.

**Includes:**

* Date
* Total number of buses operated
* Total number of students transported
* Total arrival attendance
* Total return attendance
* Morning periods total
* Evening periods total

**Grouped By:**

* Period (Fajr, Duha, Asr, Maghrib, Isha)

---

## 2.2 Daily Bus Attendance Report

**Purpose:**
Monitor attendance per bus for the day.

**Includes:**

* Date
* Period
* Bus number
* Route
* Location / Parking (A8–A11, B8–B11)
* Number of students assigned
* Present count
* Absent count
* Late count
* Excused count
* Unregistered students count

---

## 2.3 Daily Supervisor Performance Report

**Purpose:**
Track supervisor activity and workload.

**Includes:**

* Supervisor name
* Assigned location
* Number of buses supervised
* Attendance completion status per bus
* Attendance submission time
* Sync status (online / offline synced)

---

## 2.4 Daily Suspensions & Reactivations Report

**Purpose:**
Track student status changes for the day.

**Includes:**

* Student name
* Registration number
* Action type (Suspended / Reactivated)
* Reason
* Performed by (user)
* Date and time

---

## 2.5 Daily Registration Requests Report

**Purpose:**
Monitor incoming student transport requests.

**Includes:**

* Number of new requests
* Approved requests
* Rejected requests
* Pending requests
* Requests grouped by district

---

# 3. Monthly Reports

Monthly reports focus on **trend analysis, operational efficiency, and staff performance**.

---

## 3.1 Monthly Attendance Trend Report

**Purpose:**
Analyze attendance behavior over the month.

**Includes:**

* Total students transported per day
* Average attendance rate
* Average absence rate
* Attendance trends by period

---

## 3.2 Monthly Bus Utilization Report

**Purpose:**
Evaluate bus capacity and usage efficiency.

**Includes:**

* Bus number
* Route
* Average students per trip
* Peak usage days
* Underutilized buses

---

## 3.3 Monthly District Coverage Report

**Purpose:**
Assess transport demand by district.

**Includes:**

* District name
* Number of registered students
* Average daily attendance
* Number of buses serving the district

---

## 3.4 Monthly Supervisor Activity Report

**Purpose:**
Measure supervisor engagement and reliability.

**Includes:**

* Supervisor name
* Total attendance sessions handled
* On-time submissions
* Offline submissions synced
* Error or retry counts

---

## 3.5 Monthly Suspensions & Leave Report

**Purpose:**
Monitor student service interruptions.

**Includes:**

* Total suspended students
* Suspension reasons summary
* Total leave requests
* Average leave duration

---

## 3.6 Monthly Registration & Bus Assignment Report

**Purpose:**
Track growth and changes in transport usage.

**Includes:**

* New student registrations
* Students assigned per district
* Bus assignment changes
* Transfers between buses

---

# 4. Annual Reports

Annual reports support **strategic planning, budgeting, and policy decisions**.

---

## 4.1 Annual Transport Performance Report

**Purpose:**
Provide a comprehensive overview of transport operations.

**Includes:**

* Total students served annually
* Total buses operated
* Average daily attendance
* Peak operation periods

---

## 4.2 Annual Attendance Analytics Report

**Purpose:**
Identify long-term attendance patterns.

**Includes:**

* Attendance rate by period
* Absence trends
* Seasonal variations (summer program vs normal)

---

## 4.3 Annual District Demand Report

**Purpose:**
Support route planning and resource allocation.

**Includes:**

* District-wise student counts
* Growth or decline trends
* Required bus capacity recommendations

---

## 4.4 Annual Supervisor & Operations Report

**Purpose:**
Evaluate operational staffing effectiveness.

**Includes:**

* Supervisor workload summary
* Performance indicators
* Attendance compliance metrics

---

## 4.5 Annual Student Lifecycle Report

**Purpose:**
Track student engagement with transport services.

**Includes:**

* Total registered students
* New registrations
* Suspensions
* Reactivations
* Leaves taken

---

## 4.6 Annual Special Operations Report

**Purpose:**
Review non-standard operations.

**Includes:**

* Bus merging periods
* Summer program participation
* Temporary route changes
* Operational impact analysis

---

# 5. Report Features & Controls

All reports shall support:

* Date range filtering
* Export (PDF / Excel)
* Role-based access
* Drill-down from summary to details

---

## 6. Traceability

These reports align with:

* BRD reporting objectives
* FRD statistics requirements
* Offline attendance synchronization data

---

## 7. Approval

This report specification is subject to stakeholder approval prior to implementation.
